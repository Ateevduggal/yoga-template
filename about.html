    Want to Know More about AD

    AD is a group that is dedicated to providing yoga, meditation, and mindful living to our 3K+ students by our 30K+ courses through classes, workshops, and whatnot. Members can access their accounts on any device including, phones, tablets, and TVs with the help of our mobile and TV apps.
    We arrange classes for students of all levels so that they can learn yoga at their speed without any limitations or any setbacks with the help of our teachers and instructors whose knowledge has no bounds and we expect our students to aim to be like them so that they can one day teach with us on this platform only to others what they have learned through vigorous training and efforts.
    We believe that yoga is for everyone and, everyone must have this as a part of their daily routine.
    Meet the People behind AD's success
    Since 2019, we have been committed to making yoga more available and accessible to people of all ages, sizes, and levels of practice.
    Our team of producers, photographers, videographers, teachers, writers, and editors have helped shape AD so that today it is one of the leading online yoga resources and streaming video services.
    Together we cultivate a strong community with a vision to create a positive, welcoming space for people to be their most authentic selves. We aim to give our members the best yoga experience possible by offering top-quality content from expert teachers.
